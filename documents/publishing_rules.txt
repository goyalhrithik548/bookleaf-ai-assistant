BOOKLEAF PUBLISHING RULES

1. Authors must submit final manuscripts before the submission deadline.

2. Royalty payments are processed monthly.

3. Books go live within 30 days after final approval.

4. Authors can request dashboard access after onboarding.

5. Marketing add-on services include:
   - PR Campaign
   - Social Media Promotion
   - Bestseller Package

6. ISBNs are assigned automatically.

7. Authors receive one complimentary author copy.

8. Additional author copies can be purchased separately.

9. Publishing timelines may vary depending on editing status.

10. All disputes are handled through support escalation.
