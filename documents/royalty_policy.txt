
ROYALTY PAYMENT POLICY

1. Royalty payments are released on the 5th of every month.

2. Minimum payout threshold is ₹1000.

3. Payment methods:
   - Bank Transfer
   - UPI

4. Delayed payments may occur during bank holidays.

5. Royalty reports are updated weekly.

6. Pending royalties are marked as Processing.

7. Paid royalties are marked as Paid.

8. Failed payments require account verification.

9. Authors can contact support for royalty disputes.

10. Tax deductions apply according to Indian regulations.
